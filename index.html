Smart Doctor App

Smart Doctor App is the use of medical based communication and information technology to provide clinical health care from a distance that allows patients to easily book their appointment at a particular doctor related to their health issue. Such as Dental, Injury, Metal health, Muscle strain, Obesity, Arthritis, Asthma, etc. It has been used to overcome distance barriers and to improve access to medical services that would often not be consistently available in distant rural communities. Many people are suffering from several different diseases (around 95% of the world’s population). Each week, many people from those book appointments to a nearby doctor to get the treatment. A web- based consultation system which can work on low bandwidth mobile internet connection to provide online care for rural patients. This application permits user to induce instant oversight on their health problems through a wise health care application on-line.
